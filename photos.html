About Bolt 🐶

Bolt is my sweet Maltipoo — a mix of Maltese and Poodle. He's fluffy, playful, and absolutely loves cuddles.

He doesn't go on walks very often, so when I walk your dog, I give them my complete, undivided attention.

If I ever do walk Bolt at the same time, I always walk him separately so every dog gets the best experience possible.
